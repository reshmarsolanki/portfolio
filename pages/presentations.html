Presentations

Slide decks shouldn't be boring, but they are often thrown together hastily to meet a deadline. If the point of a deck is to get people to pay attention - then it's important to show how design can change the impact of a presentation. I used design to show why less can be more when design is taken into consideration. I wanted to show various ways to present information and was able to build up my skills in PowerPoint, Google Slides and Figma.
